02 · The frame

60 · 30 · 10.

The way we look at most operations. 60 percent of the work is traditional code and database plumbing. 30 percent is rule-based logic. 10 percent is genuine AI. Teams who skip the distinction put language models where Postgres would do the job faster, cheaper, and with better guarantees.

60%
Code & database

Owner onboarding. Document intake, status tracking, reminders. Mostly traditional plumbing with a thin AI layer for natural-language summaries.

30%
Rule-based logic

Dynamic pricing. Ninety percent rule-based logic sitting on top of an existing vendor. Owner dispute paths built into the rules, not bolted on.

10%
Genuine AI

Owner reporting. Data pipelines with a generative layer for the executive summary. The AI piece is small. The orchestration piece is the whole job.
